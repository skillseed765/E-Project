Brooklyn Bridge

The Brooklyn Bridge connects Manhattan and Brooklyn over the East River in New York City. Completed in 1883, it was one of the first steel-wire suspension bridges and remains an iconic architectural marvel, combining engineering ingenuity with historic significance.
