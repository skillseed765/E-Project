Golden Gate Bridge

The Golden Gate Bridge is an iconic suspension bridge connecting San Francisco to Marin County, USA. Opened in 1937, it is known for its striking orange color and Art Deco design.

Special Features:

  • Length: 2.7 km
  • Opened: May 27, 1937
  • Main Span: 1,280 meters
  • Traffic: Around 112,000 vehicles daily
